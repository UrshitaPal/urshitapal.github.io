As of Fall 2023, I am a third-year graduate student in pure mathematics at the University of Michigan. My advisor is Jenny Wilson.
I am broadly interested in topology, algebra and combinatorics. I am currently studying (co)homological stability patterns in families of groups (such as SL_nZ) and topological spaces (such as configuration spaces).

Outside of math, I enjoy swimming, hula hooping, and paper crafts.
